Distinguished Background in Federal Law

Sam Scales is an experienced attorney with a distinguished background in both the Department of Justice and public defense. This rare combination means he understands exactly how the federal government builds their cases — and how to dismantle them.

His practice focuses on protecting constitutional rights through complex federal litigation, employment matters, and regulatory cases. Every client receives the aggressive, strategic defense they deserve.
